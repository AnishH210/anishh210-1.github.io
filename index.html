Our Menu

Chicken

Saute onion and garlic in olive oil for 2 minutes.Add canned corn and cook for 5 minutes.Add salt and pepper and jalapeno brine. Add a little water and cook for another 5 minutes. Let it cool and then puree in a blender.Heat it up again add cream and butter along with grilled chicken slices.Garnish with a fried egg.Add fennel pickle (cooked in salt, chili, sugar and vinegar). Serve it in a pasta plate with a toasted garlic bread.
